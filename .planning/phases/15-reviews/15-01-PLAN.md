<objective>
Create the backend review collection system: APScheduler-based polling service that sends review requests to clients 2h after completed visits via bot, bot callback handlers for the star-rating + optional text flow, and auto-publish service for stale negative reviews after 7 days.

Purpose: Enable automatic review collection without master intervention. The bot proactively asks clients for feedback after visits, and negative reviews eventually publish even without master response.
Output: Two new APScheduler services, adapter methods for review request messages with inline star buttons, and bot callback handlers for the rating flow.
</objective>
